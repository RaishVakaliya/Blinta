Blinta is a modern social media mobile application that enables users to share image-based posts, interact with others, and stay connected through a clean and intuitive mobile experience.

## Features

- **Authentication:** Secure user authentication using Google sign-in.
- **Post Creation:** Create and share posts with images and captions.
- **Feed:** View a chronological feed of posts from other users.
- **Likes & Comments:** Interact with posts through likes and comments.
- **Likes List Modal:** Tap on the like count to see who liked a post and follow/unfollow directly from the list.
- **Bookmarking:** Save posts to a personal bookmark collection.
- **Follow System:** Follow and unfollow users to stay connected.
- **Hidden Users:** Hide posts from specific users and manage hidden users from your profile.
- **Post Management:** Delete your own posts when needed.
- **Notifications:** Receive real-time notifications for likes, comments, and follow activities.
- **User Profiles:** View and manage user profiles with profile pictures, bios, and post history.
- **Profile Post Viewer:** Open posts from profile grids to see full details (caption, likes, comments, and actions).
